## Alternative Suppliers — All-Road / O|X replacement

### LightCarbon
- Website: lightcarbon.com
- China, founded 2012
- Capacity: 2000 frames/month
- Strong gravel/all-road catalog — includes frames with downtube storage, micro suspension, T47 BB, UDH hanger
- OEM/ODM for global brands including startups
- MOQ: not published, contact required
- Relevant note: has gravel frames with in-frame storage — relevant for O|G too
- **Priority: HIGH** — good catalog fit, professional setup

### Dengfu
- Website: dengfubike.com
- China, founded 2007
- MOQ: 1 piece (very startup-friendly)
- EN/UCI certified, painting factory in-house
- Broader brand, less premium positioning than Mondince/LightCarbon
- **Priority: MEDIUM** — good for sampling and early orders, lower brand cachet

### Rinasclta
- Website: rinascltabike.com
- China (Shenzhen factory + Xiamen rim factory), founded 2011
- OEM-focused, sells to other brands not just direct
- 100% EN/UCI testing, 2-year warranty
- Good reviews on quality/detail, responsive support (24h)
- **Priority: HIGH** — professional OEM focus, good fit for small brand

### ICAN (ICANBikes)
- Website: icanbikes.com / icancycling.com
- China, founded 2009
- ~1700 frames/month, UCI certified
- OEM/ODM service, also sells consumer direct
- **Priority: MEDIUM** — solid but more consumer-facing than pure OEM
